Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best routine for the gym?

A mix of strength training, cardio, and flexibility is key. Focus on compound exercises and stay consistent!

What should beginners do at the gym?

Start with light weights, learn proper form, and don't rush. A trainer consultation can help!

What are the 10 best exercises?

Squats, Deadlifts, Push-ups, Pull-ups, Lunges, Planks, Shoulder Press, Rows, Dips, and Burpees.

How do I schedule my workout at the gym?

Plan 4-5 days/week. Alternate strength and cardio days. Stick to a consistent time if possible!

Is it better to do a full-body workout every day?

No, muscles need time to recover! Doing full-body daily can lead to overtraining and injury.

What should I do on rest day?

Active recovery! Light stretching, yoga, or a gentle walk helps muscle repair without strain.

How long should I work out each day?

30-60 minutes is ideal, depending on your goals and intensity.

What should I eat before a workout?

A light meal with carbs and protein 1–2 hours before training fuels your session best!

Workout Selection Tips:

Focus on compound lifts (squats, deadlifts, etc.).
Incorporate cardio 2-3 times a week.
Balance pushing and pulling exercises.
Don't skip flexibility/mobility work.
Train what you enjoy to stay consistent!
