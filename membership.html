Frequently Asked Questions

Which plan is right for me?

It depends on your fitness goals! Basic is for gym access, Plus adds classes, and Elite includes personal training.

Can I upgrade anytime?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade anytime by contacting the front desk.

Is there a trial?

We offer a Free First Class or Tour to try before you commit!

How do I cancel?

You can cancel anytime with a 30-day notice period via your online portal or at the gym.

Do you offer family discounts?

Yes, we offer family bundles! Ask our staff for details when signing up.

Are there hidden fees?

No hidden fees. Everything is clearly outlined in your membership agreement.

Can I pause my membership?

Yes, you can pause your membership if needed, for up to 3 months at a time.

What is included in a free consultation?

A fitness assessment with a personal trainer to evaluate your starting point and goals!
