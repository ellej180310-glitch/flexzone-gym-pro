    Top 5 Nutrition Tips for Fitness Success

    • 🥗 Focus on whole foods and lean proteins.
    • 💧 Stay hydrated: drink at least 8 glasses of water daily.
    • 🕒 Time your meals around workouts for better recovery.
    • 🍎 Include fruits and vegetables at every meal.
    • ⏳ Avoid skipping meals to maintain energy levels.

    Nutrition FAQ

    How do I book a session with Emily?

    Simply visit our contact page and request a nutrition consult!

    Are FlexFuel Bar snacks available to all members?

    Yes! You can purchase them anytime during gym hours.

    What certifications does Emily have?

    Emily holds a Certified Nutrition Specialist (CNS) and ACE Health Coach certification.

    What types of diets do you offer?

    FlexZone provides guidance for vegetarians, vegans, keto, paleo, and more!

    Do I need to follow a strict meal plan?

    No. We focus on balance and flexibility for sustainable nutrition.

    Are the plans tailored for weight loss or gain?

    Yes. Each plan is adjusted to your goals: weight loss, maintenance, or gain.

    Do you provide dietary supplements?

    We offer shakes, bars, and natural supplements at the FlexFuel Bar.
